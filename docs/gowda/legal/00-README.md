## Checklist before using Gowda data in a product or publication

1. **Cite** both the 2025 neuroprosthesis paper and OSF dataset DOI ([04-attribution-citation.md](04-attribution-citation.md)).
2. **Review** OSF project license / terms on [YM5JD](https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/YM5JD) at time of use ([02-data-license-osf.md](02-data-license-osf.md)).
3. **Do not** imply UC Davis / author endorsement of derivative hardware or clinical devices without permission.
4. **Separate** OpenAlterEgo validation numbers from paper PER/WER — different task and pipeline ([../openalterego/01-gap-analysis.md](../openalterego/01-gap-analysis.md)).
5. **New human recording** in your lab requires your own IRB — do not reuse paper consent for new participants ([01-ethics-irb.md](01-ethics-irb.md)).

## Paper statements (source: arXiv:2502.05762v2)

**Ethics:** Research conducted under UC Davis IRB protocol **2078695-1**, Declaration of Helsinki. Written informed consent; deidentified data publication consent.

**Conflict of interest:** H. T. Gowda and L. M. Miller are inventors on Regents of the University of California IP related to silent speech, **not presently licensed**.

**Open release:** Authors state data, code, and checkpoints are open-sourced (GitHub + OSF). Confirm license files in each repository before redistribution.
